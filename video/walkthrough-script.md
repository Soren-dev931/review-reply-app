## PRODUCTION NOTES

- **Total estimated runtime:** 4:00–4:40
- **Screen recording:** Record at 1920x1080 from live deployed app
- **Voiceover:** Record separately, edit to match screen actions
- **Music:** Subtle background track, fade in/out during intro/outro only
- **Transitions:** Simple crossfade between sections, no fancy effects
- **Captions:** Add subtitles — most people watch muted first
- **Thumbnail:** Split screen — messy reviews on left, clean AI response on right

### Sample Reviews to Use in Recording

**4-star (restaurant):**
"Food was amazing, especially the garlic butter shrimp. Only reason I'm not giving 5 stars is we waited about 40 minutes for a table on a Saturday night. Our server Marcus was fantastic though — really attentive and made great recommendations."

**1-star (dental):**
"Had an appointment at 2pm, didn't get seen until 3:15. The hygienist was rough and the front desk staff seemed annoyed when I asked about my insurance. Won't be back."

**5-star (salon):**
"Lisa did an incredible job on my balayage. I showed her a Pinterest photo and she nailed it. The salon is clean, everyone's friendly, and she finished right on time. Already booked my next appointment!"

**3-star (auto repair):**
"They fixed my transmission issue but it took 3 days longer than they originally quoted. Communication could have been better — I had to call them for updates instead of them reaching out. Work quality was fine though."
